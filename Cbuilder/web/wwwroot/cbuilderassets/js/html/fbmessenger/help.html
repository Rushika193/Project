Steps to add Facebook Messenger
Before continuing verify that you are the Admin of the page(editor or moderator cannot perform this action)
1. Login to your Facebook Page.
2. Go to Setting of Page.
3. On the left Column Click On, Messenger Platform
4. Add Your Https:// domain in Whitelist and click on Save.
5. Now get your PageID from about section of your page and copy it.
6. Go to Basic setting of this Component and paste it in PageID field.
7. Also, choose your desired color and change your greeting message at the time when user is logged in or logged out of their Facebook account.
