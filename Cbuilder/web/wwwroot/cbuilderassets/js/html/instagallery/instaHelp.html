1. Go to Insta Manage
    2. Register for new ClientID
    3. After registration, you will get a Client ID.
    4. Click Manage button (at right top of your Registerd Client details) and under Security tab, uncheck Disable implicit OAuth.
    5. Go to Get Access Token
    6. Replace the CLIENT-ID with the registered ClientID from step 3 and REDIRECT-URI with the URI you entered while registering for new ClientID in the above URL.
    7. You will be directed to the url you entered, accesstoken will be provided under parameter #access_token parameter.
    8. Enter the Access Token and number of items to fetch in the basic setting.
    9. Then hit submit button. Upon successful API connection your photos will be displayed in the view.
    10. For further details Go to Insta Help
